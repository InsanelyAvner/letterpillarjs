LetterPillar Tools Usage

Congratulations on setting up the tools! They are now ready to be used

This section will teach you how to use the tools

1. Name Edit Function

Feel like changing your username? Sure!

Navigate to your username and click on the edit icon located next to it.

Afterwards, enter your new username in the following prompt.

When you are done, press 'ok'. Enjoy :D

2. XP Edit Function

Too little XP? The solution is comming right away!

Navigate to your XP bar and click on the small plus icon located next to it.

Afterwards, enter your desired XP amount. Don't be greedy :)

When you are done, press 'ok'. Enjoy :D

3. Medals Adder Function

No medals? Want some? Here's the guide from the professionals!

Navigate to the Medals box and click on the small plus icon located next to it.

Afterwards, enter your desired medals amount. Again, don't be greedy :]

When you are done, press 'ok'. Enjoy :D

4. LetterDollars Adder Function

Last but not least, the LetterDollars Adder Function!

Navigate to the LetterDollars box and click on the tiny plus icon located next to it.

Afterwards, enter your desired LetterDollars amount. Take as much as you want :)

When you are done, press 'ok'. Enjoy :D
